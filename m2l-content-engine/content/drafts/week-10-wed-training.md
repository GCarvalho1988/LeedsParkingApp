Four weeks out.

Last long ride done — 140km on Saturday. Final big one before the taper. My body agreed, mostly.

The legs are where they need to be. The uncertainty now isn't physical — it's whether I can hold focus for 12 to 14 hours on the day. That bit you can't really train for. You find out you can, or you find out you can't.

Manchester to London isn't like Fred Whitton. Less dramatic, more sustained. No single mountain to frame the suffering neatly. Just 200 miles of road, mostly pointing south.

Four weeks. Still moving.
